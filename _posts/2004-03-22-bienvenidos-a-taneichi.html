Wow! Taneichi just got five Peruanas. They are two sisters and three daughters (one, four, and six years old), and I assume that one of the sisters is married to a Japanese man, because the girls have Japanese names. The sisters are very nice, we chatted a little in a beautifully convoluted chanpoñol*, which was fun in a brain-stretching kindof way. They just moved to an apartment in Ôsawa, and the girls will be going to Jônai. I hope this might be my in to visit the elementary school side more often, like every week. The mother has lived in Japan for nine years.

I didn't see this one coming at all; I bet my life in Taneichi just got considerably more interesting.
