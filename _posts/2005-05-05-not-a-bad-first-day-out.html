first day out
sight
magic-hour light dancing on every tiny disturbance in the water
smell
wetsuit's rubbery, wax's coconutty
sound
wave crashing over to icecream headache land
taste
salty skin
feel
escudo's heater on chilly flip-floppy feet
sand in my toes, sand in my hair, sand in my pants, sand everywhere
