warm cigarettes and statuesque poses
my hand in your pocket your hand in my heart
shoes tied together we sniff the blue roses
what brought us together will pull us apart

i, on my way with a shuffle and skip
you looked at me sideways as the room spun
next to the fire your teeth cut my lip
i saw by my eyes that it had begun

so i'll be your woman if you are my man
or maybe i'm thinking in upsidedown terms
and if it works out that we meet in japan
we'll laugh and we'll love, we'll dance and we'll learn
