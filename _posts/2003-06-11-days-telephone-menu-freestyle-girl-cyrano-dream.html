Bristol was lovely. While Minta was at the Ortho I took a nap at her house, and had a strange dream... I was using a telephone menu system, and each keyed entry would reward me with a girl doing a freestyle rap. She was really good, but after pushing one of the numbers she confided (in rhythm and rhyme, of course) that somebody else was writing the lyrics for her. I was close to making the realization that it was me that was her Cyrano, but Minta came in and dropped her keys on my stomach. If I had figured it out while still dreaming I might have gone into a coma.

Do people meet themselves in dreams?
