ya e za ku ra
spring rain leaves everything clean
and bursting with life *

Multilayer-flowered yaezakura bloom a little later than regular sakura. I'm not usually one to try and take pictures of flowers, but... the afternoon spring shower paused, leaving them covered in pretty little drops, and the sun came out to play for the magic hour... there was even a freaking rainbow. How could I not give it a shot?
