My personal web space has gone through a lot of redesigns through the years, but this could be the last. Why? It now features an aggregator that pulls feeds from all of my web postings. I will still mess with the theme, but I think I'll stick with Drupal for the backend for a while.

Drupal is by far the best CMS with which I've ever messed. Structured data is so easy with pathauto and taxonomies. Look how much Google likes the newly Drupal-powered folktunes.org. Enough geeking out about pretty URLs.

While I'm bestowing praise, Dreamhost rocks. Does anybody need a website?

I've also started the BboxX - Lessons from a Human Beatbox podcast/blog (). Simple mp3 multivocalism lessons with rhythms broken down. All for fun.
