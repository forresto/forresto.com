I have always wanted to write a program to draw a Fibonacci Spiral, and today I finally succeeded. Flash does not make it straightforward to to draw circular sections, so the curves are a little squashed, but get they get the point across. Flash chokes when the movie clips get huge, so for now I have it set up to reset itself before that. The funkiest part was to get it to resize and recenter every time.
