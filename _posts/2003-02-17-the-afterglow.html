I woke up the morning inspecting my feelings...

I had a dream with Aisling and her brother doing a Rogers/Astaire/Icedance thing in a house filled with greenery as a response to my little tapdance + acapella version of "Life Could Be a Dream."

I think the pattern is easy to spot... The day after is a daze... practical issues like getting a phone number are glazed over and all you need is to space out. The day after the day after is blue, filled with self-doubt and self-questioning.

And the third day is the test. When I woke up I felt at peace with the situation, whatever the outcome might be.
